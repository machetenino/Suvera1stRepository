Who we are

Construction management,
done properly.

Suvera is a Sydney construction management firm. We take on the project and remain answerable for how it is delivered — engaging the right consultants, designers and trades, coordinating them, and taking responsibility for the outcome.

We work across commercial fitout, workplace, retail, hospitality and industrial refurbishment. The type of project does not change how we work — the same process, the same rigour, whether it is an A-grade CBD office or a warehouse in Western Sydney.

If something changes on site — scope, programme, cost — you hear it from us before it becomes a problem.

What we do

Services

  • 01

    Commercial Fitout

    Corporate offices · A-grade · Base building +

    A-grade and commercial office environments delivered end to end. We manage the full scope — design consultants, services engineers, specialist trades — and remain the single point of accountability through to practical completion.

  • 02

    Retail & Hospitality

    Restaurants · Bars · Retail · Brand environments +

    Bars, restaurants, cafés, and retail environments. We work closely with interior designers to protect the design intent all the way through construction. Nothing is value-engineered without a conversation first.

  • 03

    Industrial Refurbishment

    Warehouses · Industrial fitout · Base building +

    Warehouse upgrades, base building refurbishments, and industrial fitouts. Often unglamorous. Always held to the same standard as our A-grade work — because the brief doesn't determine our standard. We do.

  • 04

    Workplace Fitout

    Corporate workplaces · Fitout · Refurbishment +

    Purposeful environments built for the people who use them. We manage every consultant, every trade, every interface and every timeline — so the business keeps running while we run the build.

  • 05

    Head Contracting

    Head contractor · Programme · Compliance +

    We take on head contractor responsibility for the full scope — programme, subcontractors, compliance, documentation — and remain accountable through to final handover and the defects liability period.

  • 06

    Make Good & Tenancy Works

    Make good · Tenancy works · Occupied environments +

    Make goods, base building upgrades, and works in occupied tenancies. We plan around your operations. Our construction happens inside someone's business — we treat it accordingly.
